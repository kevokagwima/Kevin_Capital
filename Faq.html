FAQ

What is a Hedge Fund?

A Hedge Fund is an alternative investment that is designed to protect investment portfolios from market uncertainity, while generating positive returns in both up and down markets. It's available only to sophisticated investors such as institutions and indidviduals with significant asset. Throughout time investors have looked for ways to maximize profits while minimizing risks.

What do hedge funds invest in?

A hedge fund can basically invest in anything... land, real estate, stocks, derivatives and currencies. Mutual funds, by contrast, have to basically stick to bonds and are usually long-only. They often employ leverage: Hedge funds will often use borrowed money to amplify their returns.

Are hedge funds a high risk?

As noted hedge funds very often use speculative investments and trading strategies. Many hedgr funds are honestly managed and balance a high risk of capital loss with a hgh potential for capital growth. The risk hedge funds incur, however, can wipe out your entire investment.

How does hedge funds make money?

Hedge funds make money by charging a management fee and a performance fee. While these fees differ by fund, they typically run 2% and 20% assets under management. Management fees: This fee is calculated as a percentage of asset under management.... This incentive fee motivates the fund to generate excess returns.

Are hedge funds legal?

Legally, hedge funds are most often set up as private invstment limited partnerships thet are open to a limited number of accredited investors and requires a large initial minimum investment.

how does a hedge fund work?

A hedge fund is a pool of money contributed by investors and run by a fund manager whose goal is to maximize returns and eliminate risk.... Regardless of the structure, the hedge fund is operated by a manager who invests the money into different assets to achieve the fund's goals

How much do you need to invest in a hedge fund?

But keep in mind that that the typical minimum hedge fund investment is between $500,000 and $1million. Most of the big successfull funds have a much higher minimal initial investment. Managers are legally allowed to accept investments from up to 35 people who aren't accredited investors.

Is a hedge fund a good investment?

Hedge funds offer diversification. Hedge funds can be a worthwhile investment because the assets allow them to diversify and provide a lack of correlation to the stock market.
